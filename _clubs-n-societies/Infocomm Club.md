## Infocomm Club

<img src="/images/CCA-04.jpg" style="width:85%">

Hua Yi’s Infocomm Club excites Hua Yians about the possibilities of infocomm through an engaging training programme. Through the trainings provided, members develop creative and entrepreneurial dare by applying infocomm skills such as technical knowledge of sound systems, coding and programming skills in school and the community.
