<h2>Principal's Message</h2>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>A warm welcome to Hua Yi Secondary School’s website!</strong>
</p>
<p>At Hua Yi, we are committed to nurturing a caring school community and
preparing our students to be future-ready. We want every student to feel
known, valued, and empowered, with the space to learn, lead, and grow.</p>
<p>Building on our strong foundation of care and character, we continue to
evolve to meet the needs of our times — helping our students find their
voice and confidence as they navigate an ever-changing world. This spirit
of growth and renewal comes to life as we look forward to a significant
milestone — our 70th Anniversary in 2026.</p>
<p>As one of Singapore’s first government-established Chinese secondary schools,
our history is a source of pride. Our 2026 school theme, <strong>“Our Legacy, Our Voice, Our Future,”</strong> celebrates
our strong heritage and reaffirms our mission to nurture Hua Yians who
embody our school values — 仁 (<strong>benevolence</strong>), 义 (<strong>justice</strong>),
智 (<strong>wisdom</strong>), 群 (<strong>togetherness</strong>).</p>
<p>This milestone year is not only a time to honour the generations of dedicated
staff, students, and alumni who have built Hua Yi’s legacy of resilience
and steady progress, it is also a reminder of the enduring spirit that
defines us. That same spirit continues to guide how we nurture our students
today: encouraging them to find their voice, live out our values, and contribute
meaningfully to the world around them.</p>
<p>We believe every student’s voice matters. At Hua Yi, students are given
meaningful opportunities to share ideas, lead change, and make a difference,
whether through class projects, Co-Curricular Activities (CCAs), or student
leadership initiatives. This spirit of empowerment is rooted in a culture
of deep care and mentorship. Our teachers guide students to take ownership
of their learning and actions, while modelling empathy and integrity. We
believe true leadership begins with care, for oneself, for others, and
for the community.</p>
<p>To prepare students for an ever-changing world, we nurture them to be
grounded in values and equipped with 21st century competencies. Guided
by our school vision — <strong>“Inculcate Values, Inspire Talents, Impact Communities”</strong> —
we seek to develop <strong>Passionate Learners, Resilient Leaders, Empathetic Contributors, and Glocalised Citizens</strong>.</p>
<p>As an <strong>Apple Distinguished School</strong> and a member of the <strong>Global AI Nexus of Schools</strong>,
we thoughtfully integrate technology into learning. Our students use digital
tools for collaboration, creativity, and innovation, while our clear school
routines help them build focus, balance, and responsibility — essential
traits for success in school and beyond.</p>
